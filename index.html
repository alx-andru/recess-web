The study

Use our app for 3 weeks.

All you have to do is to download the Recess App and use it for three weeks. Daily interaction is entirely up to you and just requires very few minutes of your time.

Track your fitness, set your goals & engage with others.

Measure your activity

We visualize your daily activity. How many steps do you walk? How many hours a day are you actually active?

Set your 7 day challenge

Take the one week challenge and motivate yourself to reach the goals you set.

Support others

You will be matched up with a "Buddy", another participant who shares the same goals. Your task is to engage and help your "Buddy" to achieve their goal.
