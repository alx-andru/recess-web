The study

Use our app for 3 weeks.

All you have to do is to download the Recess App and use it for three weeks. Daily interaction is entirely up to you and just requires very few minutes of your time.

Track your fitness, set your goals & engage with others.

Measure your activity

We visualize your daily activity. How many steps do you walk? How many hours a day are you actually active?

Set your 7 day challenge

Take the one week challenge and motivate yourself to reach the goals you set.

Support others

You will be matched up with a "Buddy", another participant who shares the same goals. Your task is to engage and help your "Buddy" to achieve their goal.

Questions

 

What is the Recess study?

The Recess study is a research study conducted at the University of Waterloo to measure behavioural change of fitness activity through social engagement.

Why is the study being done?

Increasing physical activity and reducing sedentary time is important for overall health and wellness. However, many individuals are not active enough to meet recommended guidelines for health benefits. The purpose of this study is to determine which features of a smartphone application (app, called RECESS) are effective in improving daily physical activity and reducing sedentary (sitting, lying) time.

Who will oversee the study?

The principal investigator is Pascal Poupart.

ppourpart@uwaterloo.ca

Who is eligible to participate in the study?

Anyone who has a smartphone (android or ios) with a sufficiently recent operating system (iPhone 5S or Android Jellybean) and is at least 18 years old can participate. Those who do not have their own smartphone will be excluded as the app requires a smartphone to run.

Are there any risks in participating in the study?

No.

What data will be collected as part of the study?

The RECESS app is collecting the available amount of steps and distance walked prior to one week of the app installation until the app is removed or the recess service disabled. In addition, usage behaviour of the app itself like open and close times, duration of usage and interaction with user interface elements is recorded. Every user input through the built-in messaging system is stored with a corresponding time value. The app will not be accessing any potentially sensitive information on the phone (e.g. email, web browsing data, photos), the only data accessed outside of the RECESS app is the activity data from Apple Health (iOS) or Google Health (Android).

How will the study data be used?

The study will analyze the data, apply statistical methodologies and look for correlations between social engagement through messaging and fitness activity.

What will I need to do if I participate?

Download the app and use it for three weeks.

How will my privacy be safeguarded?

At the beginning of the app a unique, random participant identifier is generated. The data is stored in a protected cloud environment. User input is solely associated with the generated id.

Can I withdraw/opt-out after enrolling?

Yes at anytime. Simply remove the app from your phone.
